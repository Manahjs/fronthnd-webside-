Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • On-time Delivery Rate: Measures the percentage of shipments delivered within the agreed-upon timeframe.
  • Order Accuracy: Assesses the accuracy of order fulfillment to ensure customers receive the correct items.
  • Inventory Turnover: Indicates how often inventory is sold and replaced over a period.
  • Warehouse Utilization: Evaluates how efficiently warehouse space is used.
  • Transportation Cost per Unit: Calculates the cost of transporting each unit of product.
  • Average Order Processing Time: Measures the time taken to process an order from receipt to shipment.
  • Order Cycle Time: Tracks the total time from when an order is placed to when it is delivered.
  • Freight Cost per Shipment: Assesses the cost of shipping each shipment.
